Meme of Thrones: Play to Earn

For gaming enthusiasts, this is our flagship product, based on Mortal Kombat with one-to-one combat. We have always observed meaningless games launched on major exchanges, but if we combine the adrenaline of gameplay with the possibility of earning money while having fun, it will be too good.

Our project is big and we have the ambition to integrate the PlayStation Store , making it accessible to all groups and classes of people on PlayStation, enabling your game wallet and accepting challenges or challenging other players. The value of a fight is defined by the challenger and accepted by whoever is confident.

The reward currency and the gameplay currency will be Thrones only, thus keeping the currency circulating, while at the same time keeping it in the wallets of investors, but eventually in the wallets of gamers, which will eventually lead to token scarcity.

Most importantly, if we reach the fundraising we need to gain visibility, we will seek a partnership with Sony to help us with the final development of the game and we will have fulfilled our duty in the crypto world, contributing to the development and bringing crypto to the daily use of gamers.

Prioritizing Game Development
and a Sustainable Ecosystem:

The Meme of Thrones team is fully focused on developing the best game possible. Therefore, the decision was made to not distribute tokens to the team. We believe that the long-term success of the game will be more beneficial for everyone involved than the immediate distribution of tokens.

Tokens will be allocated to investors, players, and exchanges, which will be crucial for the growth and sustainability of the Thrones ecosystem. When the game is ready, the team will focus on monetization through product sales and advertising, which will provide a more stable and sustainable revenue stream.

Thrones will not have token sales within the game. The Thrones token will only be available on exchanges, ensuring a fair and transparent market for all players.

We prioritize a lean and secure ecosystem, where all participants benefit from the success of the game.
