Prioritizing Game Development
and a Sustainable Ecosystem:

The Meme of Thrones team is fully focused on developing the best game possible. Therefore, the decision was made to not distribute tokens to the team. We believe that the long-term success of the game will be more beneficial for everyone involved than the immediate distribution of tokens.

Tokens will be allocated to investors, players, and exchanges, which will be crucial for the growth and sustainability of the Thrones ecosystem. When the game is ready, the team will focus on monetization through product sales and advertising, which will provide a more stable and sustainable revenue stream.

Thrones will not have token sales within the game. The Thrones token will only be available on exchanges, ensuring a fair and transparent market for all players.

We prioritize a lean and secure ecosystem, where all participants benefit from the success of the game.
